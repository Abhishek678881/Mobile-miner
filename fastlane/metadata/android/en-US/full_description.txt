<p>
  The Scala Miner is a cryptocurrency miner for all latest Android devices.
  It is very simple to setup and prevents your device from overheating.
</p>

<p>Informative interface</p>
<ul>
  <li>Notification widget with live hashrate</li>
  <li>Detailled mining log</li>
  <li>Pool & Network live statistics</li>
</ul>

<p>Pool settings</p>
<ul>
  <li>Predefined & custom mining pools</li>
  <li>Customizable payout widget</li>
  <li>Custom difficulty</li>
</ul>

<p>Temperature control</p>
<ul>
  <li>Machine-learning algorithm which prevents overheating</li>
  <li>CPU & Battery temperature settings</li> 
</ul>
